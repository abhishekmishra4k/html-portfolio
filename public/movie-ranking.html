BEST 3 MOVIES ACCORDING TO DESU

NOT IN ANY ORDER

1. Interstellar (2014) – Directed by Christopher Nolan

A visually stunning and emotionally gripping sci-fi epic, Interstellar follows a team of astronauts on a mission to find a new habitable planet as Earth faces environmental collapse. With breathtaking space sequences, deep philosophical themes about time, love, and human survival, and Hans Zimmer’s unforgettable score, this movie is a masterpiece that blends science with raw human emotion.

2. The Dark Knight (2008) – Directed by Christopher Nolan

A genre-defining superhero film, The Dark Knight presents Gotham’s battle against chaos, personified by Heath Ledger’s legendary Joker. The film explores morality, justice, and the blurred lines between good and evil, all wrapped in intense action, gripping storytelling, and incredible performances. It redefined comic book adaptations with its realism and psychological depth.

3. Shutter Island (2010) – Directed by Martin Scorsese

A psychological thriller that keeps you questioning reality, Shutter Island follows a U.S. Marshal investigating a patient’s disappearance from a mental asylum. As eerie clues unfold, the film masterfully builds suspense, leading to an unforgettable twist. With Leonardo DiCaprio’s brilliant performance and a haunting atmosphere, this movie is a mind-bending experience that lingers long after it ends.
